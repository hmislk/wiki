Then enter Patient Demographic Details such as,
* Title
* Name
* Sex
* Age
* Date of Birth
* Phone Number 
* Mobile Number
* Email, NIC / Passport No
* Address
* Area
* PHN Number
* MRN No
* Comments
* Patient Account
* Mark as foreigner
* Credit Limit

Select "Save" to register the patient.
